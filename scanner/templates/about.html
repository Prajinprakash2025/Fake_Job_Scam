Our Mission & The Problem

With the rapid growth of online job portals, the number of fraudulent job postings and internship scams has increased significantly. Fraudsters target students and job seekers with fake recruiters, misleading descriptions, and upfront payment demands, resulting in financial loss and emotional distress.

Existing platforms rely heavily on manual reporting, which is reactive rather than preventive. SafeCareer aims to change this by proactively identifying and flagging suspicious job postings using a combination of rule-based validation and machine learning techniques.

How Our Technology Works

Our intelligent backend analyzes job details such as company information, contact patterns, payment requests, and keywords. A trained Supervised Machine Learning Classification Algorithm analyzes these features and classifies postings as legitimate or potentially fraudulent based on historical scam data.
